BOAT BUILDING

The people of Terengganu have a long history of excellence in boat building. And we're not merely referring to the boats that our fishermen use; this area has also produced custom-built wooden vessels capable of traversing the open sea. Individuals from all around the globe have been known to buy boats from our boat builders. Yachts and boating aficionados from around the globe have taken notice of the Terengganu boat builders' exceptional talent and attention to detail.

Because they work entirely from memory and experience, these boat builders' abilities are highly sought after for their painstaking attention to detail. Unfortunately, the number of traditional boat builders who do not use blueprints is rapidly decreasing, as previously stated.

Making use of premium woods, specifically cengal wood, the supply of which has been known to cause order delays in the past. These boat builders will not settle for anything less than the best because their career is fashioned by craftsmanship and honesty.
