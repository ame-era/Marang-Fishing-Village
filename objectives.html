Objectives of the Fishing Villages of Marang Website

1. Community building and sharing: Showcase the fishing village's history and traditions through storytelling, photographs, or films. Also, provide villagers a forum to share their skills and crafts.

2. Boost tourism and local economy: To encourage eco-tourism among visitors, Marang's beauty, fishing culture, and homestay opportunities are highlighted. Local eateries, stores, and fishing expeditions are also listed and being promoted. Moreover, the website provides information on sustainable fishing practices and the importance of marine conservation to raise awareness and educate people.

3. Education and advocacy: Advocate for respectful interactions with the village and its surroundings through responsible tourism. Additionally. creating an opportunity to raise funds for community projects or assist fishermen in times of need.
