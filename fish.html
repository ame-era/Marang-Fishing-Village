COMMONLY CAUGHT FISH SPECIES

Mackerel (Ikan Kembung)

Mackerel is a schooling fish prized for its affordability and versatility. These sleek, silver fish with dark stripes have a mild, slightly sweet flavor and firm texture. They are a staple in Terengganu cuisine, enjoyed grilled, fried, or curried.

Sailfish (Ikan Layar)

Sailfish are not commercially fished in large quantities due to their importance in the marine ecosystem. However, they are a popular target for sport fishermen due to their impressive size, reaching lengths of over 10 feet, and their acrobatic fight when hooked.

Snapper (Ikan Snapper)

Snapper are prized reef fish known for their firm, white flesh and delicate flavor. They come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and yellow. Snapper are a versatile catch, enjoyed grilled, steamed, or baked whole.

Squid (Sotong)

Squid are cephalopod mollusks, not fish, but they are an important catch for Marang fishermen. Squid fishing typically occurs at night using jigs that resemble small fish. Their mild, slightly sweet flavor and versatility make them a popular ingredient in stir-fries, curries, and satays.
