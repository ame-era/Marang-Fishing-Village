HANDICRAFT

Terengganu is one of the several Malaysian places that stands out due to its stunning natural beauty. The arts, culture, and history of the area are equally abundant.

Home to Craftspeople

Many of Malaysia's most renowned artists and craftspeople are from this state: they excel in songket, batik, brass, wood carving, traditional carpentry and boat building. Terengganu has had a significant role in shaping and contributing to many aspects of Malay culture.

Among the many remarkable achievements of Terengganu's artisans is their mastery in hand-building boats—all without the use of nails or plans.

In addition, our songket weavers have earned renown for their creative ability to blend traditional and modern elements in elaborate patterns. Among the nation's few remaining brass industries, ours dates back 300 years. One of Malaysia's leading batik producers is Terengganu.

Popular Cuisine

Our cuisine is also much sought after by many tourists from Malaysia. Our menu features several renowned dishes, such as nasi dagang (considered the State dish, consisting of steamed glutinous and normal rice served with a tuna fish curry), sata (a type of fish paté), keropok lekor (fish sausage), keropok (fish crackers) and an assortment of traditional sweets, some of which have playful names.
