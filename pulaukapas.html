ATTRACTIONS PLACES IN MARANG, TERENGGANU

PULAU KAPAS

About Pulau Kapas

Pulau Kapas is a tranquil tropical island known for its unspoiled beauty and peaceful ambiance. It is located in Terengganu, Malaysia, off the coast of Marang. Stretching along its coastlines, the island lives up to its name with beautiful, white sand beaches. The name "kapas" comes from the Malay word for cotton. For those looking for a tranquil getaway amid the wonders of nature, this picturesque location offers a sanctuary.

Marine Life

One of Pulau Kapas' most alluring features is its crystal-clear turquoise waters teeming with vibrant marine life. Snorkelers and divers flock to the island to explore its diverse coral reefs, which host a kaleidoscope of colorful fish and other aquatic creatures. The underwater world here offers a mesmerizing glimpse into Malaysia's rich marine biodiversity, making it a prime spot for underwater enthusiasts.

Activities

Pulau Kapas offers various activities such as snorkeling, diving, kayaking, and beachcombing. Visitors can explore the island's lush forests, go birdwatching, or simply relax on the pristine beaches. The island's serene environment makes it an ideal destination for both adventure seekers and those seeking tranquility.

Accommodation

Accommodation options on Pulau Kapas range from cozy beachside chalets to eco-resorts, providing visitors with comfortable stays amidst nature. Many accommodations offer stunning views of the ocean or direct beach access, enhancing the island experience. Whether you prefer rustic charm or modern amenities, Pulau Kapas has something to suit every traveler's preferences.
